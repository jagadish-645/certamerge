# CAR Spec

The authoritative v0.1 CAR contract lives at:

```text
specs/car/CAR_SPEC.md
```

Community CARs are JSON records containing:

- change identity;
- repository identity;
- actor identity;
- risk surfaces;
- policy version;
- evidence references;
- evidence states;
- missing proof;
- verdict;
- verdict trace;
- repair missions;
- timestamps;
- integrity metadata;
- replay metadata.

The community verifier validates CAR shape and consistency without an enterprise control plane.
