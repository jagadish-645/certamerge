# Contributing

CertaMerge contributions must strengthen one of four primitives:

- evidence collection;
- risk and blast-radius classification;
- deterministic policy decision;
- signed or verifiable proof/attestation.

Before contributing behavior, add or update tests and samples.

Do not add:

- chatbot features;
- dashboard-first value;
- AI final verdicts;
- scanner replacement logic;
- telemetry by default;
- raw source-code egress;
- compliance-certification claims.

Every change should preserve the product grammar:

```text
Verdict.
Policy reason.
Missing proof.
Accountable next action.
Change Authorization Record.
```
